About Us

The Hotel Management College (THMC) is a Nationally recognised training provider registered since 1998 under ASQA masters in delivering the art of hospitality programs.
THMC provides outstanding training and education programs in the Vocational Education and Training (VET) sector from Certificate III to Diploma level courses. THMC believes all individual from diverse background can build a strong career with the right professional guide from our qualified trainers and the passionate attitude of our students towards the hospitality industry.
Together we believe with an enriched knowledge and professional career advancement all individual can get vast job opportunities in a developed society. THMC welcomes you to your lifelong career!

Practical Skills

Colleges that focus on training are geared towards preparing students for specific careers, which means they place a greater emphasis on practical skills than other types of colleges.

Flexibility

Career-Focused Curriculum

These colleges offer programs that are designed to prepare students for specific careers. They offer specialized courses that focus on the skills and knowledge needed to excel in a particular profession.

Faster Path to Employment

By focusing on career-specific training, these colleges can help students get into the workforce more quickly than traditional colleges, where students may spend several years taking general education courses before diving into their chosen field.
